Privacy Policy — Ask Noah SMS Notifications

Effective date: July 18, 2026

Ask Noah is the private, personal AI assistant operated by David Shonk (sole proprietor). This policy covers the SMS text-message notifications sent from our messaging number.

Information We Collect

We collect and store only: the mobile phone numbers of household members and family contacts who have personally asked to receive messages, their first names, and the content of the messages sent to them. This information is used solely to deliver the messages that were requested — reminders, notifications, and personal messages relayed at the account owner's request. It is stored on the account owner's private, self-hosted server.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or otherwise provide your mobile phone number or messaging consent information to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third parties.

Message Frequency and Rates

Message frequency varies and is recurring: recipients typically receive a few messages per week, only when they have requested them or when a reminder they asked for comes due. Message and data rates may apply.

Opting Out

Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages. Reply HELP for help. You may also simply tell the account owner directly.
