How Recipients Opt In — Ask Noah SMS

Effective date: July 19, 2026

Ask Noah is the private, personal AI assistant of David Shonk (sole proprietor). This page documents the complete consent experience for the SMS program. There is no public sign-up: the only recipients are the account owner himself and household/family members he personally knows.

Opt-In Method 1: Text START (primary)

Anyone wishing to receive messages texts the keyword START to +1 334 360 9525. The system records the opt-in and replies with the program disclosure message shown below. This path is carrier-verifiable: the opt-in request exists as an inbound message on the messaging platform's own logs.

START

Opt-In Method 2: Verbal request to the account owner

  1. Request: A household or family member personally asks David Shonk — in person or by phone — to receive messages from his assistant. Consent is specific to this SMS program, is never bundled with any other agreement, and is never a condition of any purchase.
  2. Contact entry: David adds that person as a named contact in the assistant's settings. The assistant cannot text anyone who is not in this contact list, and every outbound message must additionally be individually approved by David before it sends.
  3. Disclosure on first message: The FIRST text message a new recipient receives automatically includes the program disclosures. It reads:
Hi — this is Noah, David Shonk's personal assistant. You asked David to receive messages from me. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.

The disclosure text above is generated by the assistant software and shown here verbatim as hosted evidence of the consent experience.

Opting Out

Reply STOP at any time (handled automatically at the carrier level), reply HELP for help, or simply tell David — he removes the contact and the assistant can no longer text them.
